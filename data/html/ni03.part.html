Sonic Mania Plus has made Sonic great again, it has delivered to the fans massively by remaking classic levels such as Green Hill Zone, Chemical Plant, Flying Battery and more. The developers also made brand new levels for this game including Studiopolis, Press Garden and Titanic Monarch, which have all unique environments, enemies, and items that players can explore
Sonic Mania Plus features an encore mode which enables the player to play as two new characters: Ray and Mighty who have been forgotten by mainstream fans but classic Sonic fans will know exactly who they are. This new mode features new environments and backgrounds as well as new cutscenes in the game, which brings a whole new experience to the game.

Score: 10/10
