Sonic Forces is a high speed platformer which features an option to customise your own character that will help Sonic and the gang in this new adventure. This series also features a brand new antagonist into the series: Infinite, who has the power to manipulate reality using the Phantom Ruby.
The modern Sonic sections are vey fun to play as the environments are fun to blast through, but they end way too soon, which is a shame because it's over by the time you get into the level. Additionally, you can complete the whole game in around 2 hours which is really short considering the game was in development for nearly 4 years.
Overall, this game is fun if you're a sonic fan, but if you want to play a good Sonic game, just play Sonic Mania.

Score: 6/10
